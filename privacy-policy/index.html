JajanTracker Privacy Policy

last updated March 16, 2025

Thank you for using JajanTracker. Your privacy is our priority. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using the JajanTracker app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to ensure our service runs smoothly:

  • Account Information
     Email address and name you used when registering.
  • Device Information
     Device model, operating system, and unique identifiers.
  • Activity Data
     Transaction records and feature usage in the app.
  • Advertising Data
     Information from Google AdMob to display relevant ads.

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide a smooth and personalized app experience.
  • Help you track spending by categorizing transactions.
  • Show relevant ads through Google AdMob.
  • Improve features based on usage data.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share data with:

  • Google AdMob
     To deliver relevant advertisements.
  • Authorities
     If required by law or to protect users' rights.

Learn more about how Google handles data in their Privacy Policy.

4. Your Rights as a User

You have the right to:

  • Manage ad preferences through your device settings.
  • Delete your account via app settings on iOS or by contacting us.
  • Opt out of ad tracking via your device or Google account privacy settings.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely free from risk.

6. Policy Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify you through the app or website if there are significant changes.
