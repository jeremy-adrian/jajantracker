1. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • What is JajanTracker?
    JajanTracker is an expense tracker app designed to help you manage your finances easily and efficiently.
  • How do I add a spending activity?
    To record a spending activity, first create a budget in the Wallet menu. Once the budget is created, the Activity menu will be available to record your expenses. The app will automatically categorize the transactions based on the appropriate category.
  • Is my data secure?
    Yes, we implement the best security measures to protect your personal data.
